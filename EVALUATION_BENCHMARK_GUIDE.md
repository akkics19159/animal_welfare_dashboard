# Benchmark Guide

## Vision benchmark

Use the vision benchmark to evaluate object detection, counting, and behaviour recognition outputs.

## Audio benchmark

Use the audio benchmark to evaluate sound classification, distress detection, and temporal alarms.

## Sensor benchmark

Use the sensor benchmark to validate normalization, anomaly detection, trend detection, and calibration.

## Fusion benchmark

Use this benchmark to compare weighted, early, late, attention, and future transformer fusion strategies.

## Welfare reasoning benchmark

Use this benchmark to score rule coverage, contradiction detection, explanation completeness, and human agreement.
