# Temporal Welfare Analysis

## Principle
Welfare is not assessed per isolated frame only. Temporal persistence, escalation, and recovery are incorporated.

## Temporal Signals
- behaviour persistence
- recovery trend
- escalation trend
- repeated distress frequency
- event duration/frequency

## Outputs
- `temporal_welfare_state.state`
- `temporal_welfare_state.trend`
- `temporal_welfare_state.timeline`
- fusion `temporal_context.welfare_timeline`
